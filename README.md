# mmaughan0.github.io
This is the academic website for Matthew Maughan, projected to receive his PhD in Health Services Research/Health Economics at Brown University in the spring of 2028. His interests include healthcare pricing and its interaction with both provider and insurer market structure.

He has published peer reviewed work in both JAMDA and Health Affairs, public facing commentary in the Dispatch, and as a trainee at Brown School of Public Health's Center for Advancing Health Policy through Research (CAHPR), he is heavily interested in translating research to evidence based policy.
